JQuery Functions

Click

When the button is clicked, this paragraph will turn red and the font will get bold!

Hide

When the button is clicked, this paragraph will turn be hidden immediately. Hide with no parameters is the easiest way to hide elements.

Adding a duration parameter to the hide method turns it into an animation. When clicked, paragraph will be "sucked up" from bottom left corner slowly.

Show

When the button is clicked, this paragraph will turn be shown immediately. Show with no parameters is the easiest way to hide elements.

Adding a duration parameter to the show method turns it into an animation. When clicked, paragraph will be "spewed forth" from bottom left corner quickly.

Toggle

When the button is clicked, this paragraph will toggle between showing or hiding.

slideDown

When the button is clicked, height of .box is animated to reveal itself. Looks like it's sliding down.

slideUp

When the button is clicked, height of .box is animated to hide itself. Looks like it's sliding up.

slideToggle

When the button is clicked, height of .box is animated to hide/show itself. It combines the two methods above.

fadeIn

When button is clicked, .box will fade into view from nothing to opaque.

fadeOut

When button is clicked, .box will from opaque to nothing.

addClass

When button is clicked, .box will have another class added to it, making background color cadet blue.

before

When button is clicked, a blue box will be inserted before the yellow box.

after

When button is clicked, a blue box will be inserted after the yellow box.
